Who we are

VolunteerNow is a service which allows people to skip the time investment and hassle for finding a volunteer host. Finding opportunities to volunteer can be very time consuming and difficult process. Our service fixes this problem by providing cloud SMS services available to everyone; This allows our service to stay up and provide hosts volunteers as well as volunteer hours available to volunteers
